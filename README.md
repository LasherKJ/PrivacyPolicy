# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to all applications and tools produced by this developer. These applications are designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server or database under our control.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or process any of the following on our servers or in any database we operate:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Usage data or analytics
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Financial or payment information
- Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

---

## 2. Local Data Storage

Some applications may store data **locally on your device** (e.g., in local storage, browser storage, a local file, or on-device databases) in order to provide application functionality such as saving preferences or caching results. This data:

- **Never leaves your device** to any server or database we control.
- Is accessible only to you on your device.
- Can be deleted by you at any time by clearing the application's local data or uninstalling the application.

We have no access to any data stored locally on your device.

---

## 3. Third-Party APIs and HTTPS

Some applications communicate with **third-party APIs** to provide their core functionality (e.g., fetching data, performing lookups, or integrating external services). All such communication:

- Is conducted exclusively over **encrypted HTTPS connections**.
- Uses only the data necessary for the specific request you initiate.
- Is subject to the **privacy policies of the respective third-party API providers**.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services whose APIs are used by a given application. We do not control the data practices of third-party providers.

---

## 4. No Server-Side Data Storage

We do **not** operate any backend server, database, or data pipeline that receives or retains your personal data. No information you enter into our applications is transmitted to or stored in any database under our ownership or control.

---

## 5. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data:

- All API calls are made over **HTTPS** to prevent interception in transit.
- No sensitive data is logged or transmitted to any server we control.
- Local data remains on your device under your control.

While we strive to use secure practices, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of our applications, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our applications or practices. When we do, we will update the **"Last Updated"** date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

---

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please open an issue in the relevant application's repository on GitHub.

---

*This privacy policy is intended as a generic policy covering all applications produced by this developer. Individual applications may include supplementary privacy disclosures where applicable.*
